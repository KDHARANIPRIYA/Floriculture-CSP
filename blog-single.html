Various nurseries:

  • Nandanavanam Nursery, etc

A nursery is a place where young plants and trees are cultivated and grown.It is a type of agricultural facility where plants are propagated, fostered, cultivated, and sold for use in a personal garden or for commercial purpose.

“Like people, plants respond to extra attention.”
- H. Peter Loewer.

This is a blog representing the detailed information over a particular and selected nursery in Vijayawada named Vijaya Durga nursery
There were a lot huge varieties of flowers and plants in the nursery and it was like a heart blooming moment and they were almost commonly grown and cultivated plants (both in the houses and farms).

Specialities :

There were some specialities we noticed in this nursery before jumping into the survey and they are ;

  • Huge varieties of Plants available
  • Plants available at fair Prices
  • Open 24 hours
  • Fruit Plants
  • Types of plants

More about it

The most significant work i noticed - "plant classification".
They were so generous and kind, taking good care of plants and flowers like babies :)

They made me understand the major factors before buying a plant from a nursery;
Examine the plants: When you are choosing a plant, or have chosen a particular plant, pick some other plants of the same species and compare all of them. It will help to choose the best and healthy plant out of them.

Analyze the leaves: Examine the leaves is the best idea to find out the healthy plant. The leaves of a healthy plant would look fresh, not yellow or witted. However, 2 or 3 witted or yellow leaves is completely okay, if the rest of the plant is fresh.

Look for bugs: You should thoroughly examine the leaves over and underneath for pests or bugs. A healthy plant should look clean. Avoid the plant which has black spots or burning spots in the leaves. Also, check the soil as well to find out if there is any fungus or not.

Examine the soil: Most of us do not examine the soil before buying a plant, but examining the soil is extremely essential. Because the roots may have tiny white bugs which are next to impossible to find out. To have a healthy plant, you need to check the plant’s root. To check the roots, loose the outer soil of the plant and gently lift the plant out of the plot. You can also ask the nursery worker to help you.
