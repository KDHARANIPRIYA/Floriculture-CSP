Detailed overview:

Periwinkles often bloom from spring to autumn depending on the climate, and they grow to be approximately 15 centimetres in length. Theyâ€™re able to spread using rooting shoots that are long and trailing.
While the periwinkle blooms best when in the sun, they can grow happily in shady spots too. When it comes to choosing where to plant periwinkle, look for an area with no other plants, as they tend to crowd out any other flowers.
You can grow the periwinkle plant using seeds, although this takes quite a bit of time and patience. The easiest and quickest way to grow periwinkle is to use cuttings, nursery transplants or seedlings.
